# Chapter 02 — Risk Fundamentals

## Overview

Introduces the foundational concepts of risk management and demonstrates their practical application through a structured risk assessment exercise. The chapter applies these concepts within the context of a fictional organization, **Mandavi FinTech Pvt. Ltd.**, created solely for educational and portfolio purposes.

---

## Topics Covered

* Asset, Threat, Vulnerability, and Risk
* Relationships between Assets, Threats, Vulnerabilities, and Risks
* Likelihood and Impact
* Risk Scoring Methodology
* Inherent Risk vs. Residual Risk
* Risk Appetite vs. Risk Tolerance
* Risk Universe

---

## Practical Exercise

Developed a structured **Risk Scenario Assessment** for **Mandavi FinTech Pvt. Ltd.**, identifying and evaluating **15 realistic business risks** across four domains:

* IT
* People
* Physical
* Third-Party

Each scenario includes:

* Asset Identification
* Threat Identification
* Vulnerability Assessment
* Likelihood Rating
* Impact Rating
* Risk Score
* Suggested Security Controls

---

## Deliverables

* **Risk Scenario Assessment (Excel)**
* **Risk Scenario Assessment (PDF)**

The assessment documents 15 business risk scenarios using a qualitative **3×3 risk matrix** to evaluate likelihood and impact.

---

## Risk Scoring Methodology

| Likelihood | Value | Impact | Value |
| ---------- | ----: | ------ | ----: |
| Low        |     1 | Low    |     1 |
| Medium     |     2 | Medium |     2 |
| High       |     3 | High   |     3 |

**Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact**

| Score | Risk Level |
| ----: | ---------- |
|   1–2 | Low        |
|   3–4 | Moderate   |
|     6 | High       |
|     9 | Critical   |
