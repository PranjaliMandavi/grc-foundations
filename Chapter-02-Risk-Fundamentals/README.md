# Chapter 02 – Risk Fundamentals

## Objective

Learn the core concepts of cybersecurity risk management by identifying and assessing business risks in a fictional e-commerce company.

## Concepts Covered

- Asset
- Threat
- Vulnerability
- Risk
- Likelihood
- Impact
- Risk Score
- Inherent Risk
- Residual Risk
- Risk Appetite
- Risk Tolerance

## Deliverables

- Risk-Scenarios.xlsx
- Risk-Scenarios.pdf

## Risk Scoring Method

| Likelihood | Value |
|------------|-------|
| Low | 1 |
| Medium | 2 |
| High | 3 |

| Impact | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Low | 1 |
| Medium | 2 |
| High | 3 |

Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact

## Learning Outcome

This chapter demonstrates the ability to identify assets, threats, vulnerabilities, and perform a basic qualitative risk assessment.
